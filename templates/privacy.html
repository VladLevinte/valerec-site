Privacy Policy

Vale Recruitment (“we”, “us”) collects and processes personal information submitted via our website registration and contact forms. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

What we collect

When you register, we may collect details such as your name, contact information, location, trade, tickets/certifications and any documents you upload (e.g. CV, tickets).

Why we collect it

We use this information to contact you about work opportunities, assess suitability for roles, and manage recruitment processes.

How we store your data

Information submitted through our website is stored securely and accessed only by authorised staff. Documents uploaded are used only for recruitment purposes.

Sharing your data

We may share relevant details with clients where required to apply for or confirm work opportunities. We do not sell personal data.

How long we keep it

We keep personal data only for as long as needed for recruitment purposes, or as required by legal or regulatory obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your personal data. You can also object to processing in certain circumstances.

Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us at:

Email: info@valerecruitment.co.uk
